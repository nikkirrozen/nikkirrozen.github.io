He spoke of eloquence like Frank Ocean. Calming, inviting, reflective is who he was. His thoughts were like Pink + White being serenaded to you on a cool spring night. So much to think and so much to say, he filled the room in your head with everything he is. He dressed you in his style from head to toe, his wardrobe spread out in this room. I Hate You

I hate you. I hate how you came into my life unexpectedly and so effortlessly embraced me both mentally and emotionally. I hate how you made me think you were different from the tens of guys I come across, all saying the same thing and all looking at me the same way. I hate how I started to see a life with you and ease up, thinking "No, I'll be okay." I hate how I let my guard down and I shut out every other guy for you. I hate how I built up such expectations, only to be heavily disappointed. That's the thing. What I really hate is just how much I would do this all over. I would do it all over again if it meant another day with you.
